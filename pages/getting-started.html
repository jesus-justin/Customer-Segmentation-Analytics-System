Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to unlock powerful customer insights and drive data-driven business decisions.

1

Prepare Your Data

Get your customer data ready in CSV format. Include numeric features like Age, Annual Income, Spending Score, and more.

2

Upload Your File

Click upload on the dashboard and drag-and-drop your CSV. Our system automatically validates and preprocesses your data.

3

Analyze & Optimize

Let our AI find the optimal number of segments using silhouette analysis and advanced machine learning algorithms.

4

Explore Insights

Interactive visualizations show segment profiles, patterns, and correlations. Get actionable recommendations for each segment.

5

Export & Share

Download results in CSV or JSON format. Create comprehensive HTML reports to share with your team and stakeholders.

6

Take Action

Use insights to create targeted marketing campaigns, improve customer retention, and optimize business strategy.
