Getting Started

Follow these simple steps to start analyzing your customer data with our powerful segmentation platform.

Step 1: Prepare Your Data

Get your customer data ready in CSV format. Include numeric features like Age, Annual Income, Spending Score, etc.

Step 2: Upload Your File

Click the upload button on the dashboard and drag-and-drop your CSV file. Our system will automatically validate and preprocess it.

Step 3: Analyze Clusters

Let our AI find the optimal number of customer segments using advanced machine learning algorithms.

Step 4: Review Results

Explore interactive visualizations and get actionable insights for each customer segment. Export results for further analysis.

Step 5: Take Action

Use the insights to create targeted marketing campaigns and improve your business strategy.
