üìã How to Use the Application

  1. Access the Analytics Dashboard
    Click on the "Analytics Dashboard" link above or navigate to http://localhost:5000/analytics
  2. Upload Your CSV File
    Drag and drop your customer data CSV file or click to browse. Sample datasets are available in the data/ folder.
  3. Run Clustering Analysis
    The system will automatically preprocess your data and suggest optimal cluster numbers. Click "Run Clustering" to segment your customers.
  4. View Results
    Explore interactive visualizations, cluster profiles, and business recommendations.
  5. Export Results
    Download your clustered data as CSV or generate HTML reports for sharing.
